about me

I'm a 23-year-old front-end developer and graphic designer from Antwerp, Belgium. Here you can find some information about me and my work. I do websites, logos, branding and other designy stuff.

Since I was 15 years old I'm in love with graphic design.
First I started to teach myself the basics of photo manipulation and soon after I learned to design 2D and 3D logos. I pursued my passion and last year I graduated with honors from the AP university college of Antwerp as a crossmedia designer.
Currently I'm studying computer science at the Univerity of Antwerp to further understand the fundamentals of programming, so I'm ready for the future as a front-end developer and all-round crossmedia designer.
